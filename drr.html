Executive Director, Rotary Fellowship of Camping 2025-26

Rtn. Irene's leadership is rooted in bold ideas and community-first innovation. Her journey in Rotary demonstrates the power of service, mentorship, and dedication to creating lasting impact in our community.

Membership

Membership RFC is open to any interested individual participant and offered an annual membership fee of $30. We inducted over 150 Members who have fully subscribed their annual dues andcontinue to encourage others to join us because this will help us in doing much for our communities. However, lifetime membership may be offered at the discretion of the Rotary Fellowship’s Board of Directors.

My Chairperson Vision

We are only as strong as our commitment to service and our connections with one another. That's why I'm focusing on three key areas during my Chairpersonship:

"True leadership is not about position, but about creating opportunities for others to grow and make a difference."

My approach is built on the belief that every member has unique talents to contribute. By fostering an inclusive environment and providing the right support systems, we can achieve remarkable things together.

My Focus Areas

Community Impact

Member Development

Sustainable Growth

Developing systems and processes that ensure our club's longevity and continued success for years to come.

Rotary Fellowship of Camping

Rotary Fellowship of Camping is a group of friends, Rotarians and family that share a common passion of Camping and fun. It is built on a promise of making new friends, bonding and network through fun around the world but most importantly explore a hobby of Camping while serving the communities around the world.
